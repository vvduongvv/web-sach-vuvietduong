Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

❓

Is BookVoyage free to use?

Yes! BookVoyage is completely free to use. Browse and read over 100 books across 12 categories — no subscription required.

❓

How do I create an account?

Simply click the "Register" button and fill in your details. You can register as a Student, Teacher, or General Reader. It only takes a minute!

❓

Can I download books for offline reading?

Currently, all books can be read directly on our website. We are working on adding offline reading features in the future.

❓

How can I request a specific book?

Use the contact form above or email us at content@bookvoyage.com with your book request. We try to add requested books within 2 weeks.
